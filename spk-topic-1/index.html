<Personal Experience>

 

* Question Type

– 개인의 경험 및 생각을 묘사하는 문제이다!

– 취미, 사람, 장소, 음식 등 (80%)

– 준비 시간 15초, 답안 시간 45초

 

* Model Questions

– Talk about a book you have read that was important to you for some reason. Explain why the book was important to you. Give specific examples to explain your answer.

– Choose a teacher you admire and explain why you admire him or her. Please include specific examples and details in your explanation.

– Where would you like to go on your next vacation? Choose a place and explain why you would like to go there. Please include specific examples and details in your explanation.

 

* Keys to the Questions

– 하나의 답 & 두개의 이유 + 각각의 설명

[Answer] – [1st reason] – [Explanation] – [2nd reason] – [Explanation]

– 미리 답을 정해놓을 것 (e.g. 취미, 사람, 장소, 음식 등)

– 뻔한 이유 및 설명도 좋다! (창의적 X, 진실성 X)

 

  1. Who do you go for advice when you’re in trouble and why?

    I go to my father for advice when I’m in trouble.
    There are two reasons for this.
    The first reason is that he listens to me and understands my problems well.
    He has taught me so many things that have made me who I am now.
    If it hadn’t been for him, I would have been a very different man.
    The second reason is that he has a lot of experiences, so he has solutions about everything.
    I can talk to him about anything and he is always willing to help me whenever he can.
    So, I still go to him for advice when I’m in trouble.
    For these reasons, I’d say my father is the best adviser in my life.

  17. Describe the most important characteristics for a teacher to possess, including details and examples.

    In my opinion, the most important characteristics for a teacher to possess is patience.
    There are two reasons for this.
    The first reason is that it takes patience to teach.
    Students may ask a lot of questions or they may have a hard time understanding what they are learning.
    In either case, teachers need to have patience.
    The second reason is that when students make mistakes, teachers should not punish them.
    But rather, teachers should encourage them to keep trying.
    And to do that, teachers need patience.
    For these reasons, I think teachers need to be patient.

  27. <<allow 활용하기>>
    something allows us to V
    => reading allows us to be smart / eating vegetables allows us to be healthy

    <<관계 대명사로 명사 수식하기>>
    명사 + 관계대명사 + (주어) + 동사
    => a car that I bought / a car that runs fast / a car that is expensive / a book that I read / a book that touched me / a book that is interesting

    <<조건절 활용하기>>
    If + 주어 + 동사, 주어 + can, may, will + 동사
    => If you know how to use smartphones, you can not only talk people on the phone, but also take pictures, watch movies and play games anywhere anytime.

    <<가주어 활용하기>>
    To talk in class is rude. => It is rude to talk in class.
    To study English makes me happy. => It makes me happy to study English.

    <<의문사 + to부정사 활용하기>>
    what to say / where to go / how to fix my car

    <<What 절 활용하기>>
    what I do / what I eat / what cause the accident / what inspired me

    <<who 절 활용하기>>
    who I like / who I saw / who called me / who gave me a present

    <<가정법 과거완료 활용하기>> => 이대로만 사용할 것!
    If it hadn’t been for ~, I (wouldn’t / would) have p.p.
    If it hadn’t been for my father, I would have been a very different man.

    <<the way 활용하기>>
    the way + 주어 + 동사 (~하는 방법)
    => the way I walk / the way I drive / the way I sing
